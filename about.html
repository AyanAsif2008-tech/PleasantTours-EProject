About Us

Pleasant Tour is your trusted travel partner, offering unforgettable journeys to breathtaking destinations. From relaxing beach holidays and scenic hill stations to cultural heritage tours and adventure trips, we are committed to providing comfortable, affordable, and well-planned travel experiences. Our goal is to turn every trip into a pleasant memory with excellent service and customer satisfaction.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide reliable, affordable, and high-quality travel services that create memorable experiences for every traveler. We aim to make trip planning easy, smooth, and stress-free through modern technology and personal care.

Our Vision

Our vision is to become a trusted travel partner, inspiring people to explore the world with confidence. We strive to connect travelers with beautiful destinations while delivering comfort, value, and happiness on every journey.

Our Journey

The journey of Pleasant Tours began with a simple dream to make travel easy, affordable, and memorable for everyone. What started as a small idea has grown into a trusted travel agency, helping countless travelers explore beautiful destinations with comfort and confidence. Over the years, we have proudly served families, individuals, and groups by providing reliable travel solutions, customized tour packages, and dedicated customer support. Each trip we plan reflects our passion for excellence and our commitment to customer satisfaction. Our growth is built on trust, hard work, and the smiles of our happy clients. Every journey we organize teaches us something new and inspires us to improve our services continuously. Today, Pleasant Travel & Tours stands as a symbol of quality, reliability, and care, And this is just the beginning. We look forward to creating many more unforgettable travel stories together.
